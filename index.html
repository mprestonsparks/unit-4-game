Crystal Collector

Rules:

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number.
If your total score exceeds the target number, you lose.

Each crystal's value is hidden until clicked.

The target number and crystals are given new random values at the beginning of each game.
